# ⚡ Elpeda Protocol

### Intent-Native DeFi Yield Intelligence Layer on Solana
**Built for the Buildifi Hackathon**

Elpeda is a production-grade, autonomous yield optimization protocol that leverages AI to simplify DeFi for everyone. Move beyond complex manual positioning and let Elpeda's **Intent Engine** navigate Solana's liquidity for you.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🧠 AI Intent Engine
Stop juggling multiple protocols. Simply type your financial goals in natural language (e.g., *"Show me the best risk-adjusted yield for my SOL"*) and Elpeda's AI will analyze 18+ strategies across 6 vaults to recommend the perfect position.

### 🏦 The Vault Universe
Elpeda offers 6 specialized vaults traversing the Solana DeFi ecosystem:
- **Stable Yield (epUSD):** 18.4% APY via Orca CLMM + Kamino.
- **SOL Maximizer (epSOL):** 34.7% APY via Jito, Drift, and Raydium.
- **DeFi Alpha (epDEFI):** 67.2% APY high-frequency funding arbitrage.
- **BTC Hedge (epBTC):** 12.8% APY delta-neutral wBTC strategies.
- **Meme Alpha (epMEME):** 142.5% APY BONK/WIF/POPCAT liquidity provision.
- **RWA Yield (epRWA):** 7.8% APY exposure to Treasury Bills via Ondo.

### 💱 Elpeda Swap
An integrated institutional-grade exchange powered by Jupiter, featuring:
- **Best-in-class pricing** across all Solana DEXs.
- **Multi-hop route visualization.**
- **Price impact protection** and slippage controls.

### 📡 Live Intelligence Feed
Monitor protocol activity in real-time. Elpeda's transparency layer shows every rebalance, harvest, and compounding event with AI-generated reasoning.

### 🗳️ $ELP Tokenomics & Governance
$ELP is the heartbeat of the protocol:
- **Governance:** Vote on quorums for new strategy additions.
- **Fee Utility:** Stake $ELP for performance fee discounts.
- **Dynamic Rewards:** Boost your vault yields with $ELP staking.
